Empirici Academy x DataCamp Donates — Free DataCamp Scholarships Now Available

📅 November 2025 📚 Partnership Announcement ⏱️ 5 min read

Empirici Academy partners with DataCamp Donates to provide free learning opportunities

We're excited to announce that Empirici Academy has partnered with DataCamp Donates to provide motivated learners with free, one-year access to DataCamp's complete learning platform.

Whether you're a student, career changer, or self-learner, this scholarship gives you the tools to build job-ready data skills in Python, R, SQL, Power BI, machine learning, and more.

💡 Each scholarship provides access normally valued at $399 USD per year, completely free to accepted learners.

About the Partnership

DataCamp is a leading online platform for data science, analytics, and AI education, offering interactive courses, hands-on projects, and career tracks. DataCamp Donates is their social impact program that partners with educational organizations to provide free access to learners who might not otherwise afford it.

Through this partnership, Empirici Academy can distribute one-year DataCamp licenses to selected applicants who demonstrate motivation and commitment to learning data skills. This is not temporary access like DataCamp's recent Free Access Week promotion. Scholars receive a full year to complete courses, earn certifications, and build their portfolio.

What You Get

Access to 400+ courses

Hands-on projects and exercises

Accepted scholars receive full access to DataCamp for one year, including:

  • Complete course catalog — All courses, career tracks, and skill assessments across Python, R, SQL, Excel, Power BI, Tableau, and more
  • Hands-on learning — Browser-based coding exercises, real-world projects, and interactive challenges
  • Professional certifications — Industry-recognized certificates for completed tracks and assessments
  • DataLab IDE — Cloud-based development environment for your own data projects
  • Community support — Access to Empirici Academy's learning community and guidance

Earn industry-recognized certificates that boost your resume

Who Should Apply

This scholarship is for motivated individuals who will actively use the platform to develop career-ready skills. We welcome applications from:

  • University students and recent graduates seeking to add data skills to their degree
  • Job seekers and career changers moving into data, analytics, or tech roles
  • Early-career professionals looking to upskill or transition within their field
  • Self-learners and freelancers building a data science portfolio
  • Anyone committed to investing time in structured learning over the full year

Scholarships open to learners from all backgrounds and experience levels

No prior experience is required. DataCamp has beginner-friendly tracks that start from zero. What matters most is your motivation and willingness to commit consistent time to learning.

Ready to Apply?

How to Apply

The application is straightforward and designed to understand your goals and commitment:

  1. Basic information — Provide your contact details, current occupation or study status, and location
  2. Learning goals — Tell us what you want to learn (Python, SQL, data analysis, machine learning, etc.) and why
  3. Motivation statement — Write 4-6 sentences about your goals, what you plan to accomplish with DataCamp access, and how this fits your career plans
  4. Optional materials — You may upload a CV or portfolio if you have one, but this is not required

After submitting, Empirici Academy will review applications and contact selected candidates by email within 1-2 weeks. Accepted scholars will receive instructions for activating their DataCamp access through our partner group.

Selection Criteria

We select scholars based on:

  • Clear learning goals — Specific plans for what you want to learn and achieve
  • Demonstrated commitment — Evidence you'll use the full year of access productively
  • Fit with DataCamp content — Your goals align with the courses and tracks DataCamp offers
  • Equity considerations — We aim to support learners from diverse backgrounds and circumstances

Priority is given to applicants who clearly articulate their goals and show they've thought about how DataCamp fits into their learning path.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long will I have access?

Accepted scholars receive one full year of access from the date their license is activated. You can learn at your own pace throughout the year.

Is this the same as DataCamp's Free Access Week?

No. Free Access Week was a temporary public promotion that DataCamp ran in early November. This scholarship provides selected learners with a full year of sustained access through the DataCamp Donates program.

Do I need prior experience in programming or data?

No. DataCamp offers beginner tracks that assume no prior knowledge. We expect you to be motivated and commit regular time to working through courses, but you can start from zero.

What if I don't complete everything in one year?

You have one year to make as much progress as possible. Focus on completing career tracks and earning certifications that matter most for your goals. Any certificates you earn remain yours permanently.

How will I know if I'm accepted?

Empirici Academy will email all applicants with a decision within 1-2 weeks of application. Accepted scholars will receive detailed activation instructions and an invitation to join our DataCamp learning group.

Can I apply if I'm not a current Empirici Academy student?

Yes. This scholarship is open to motivated learners regardless of whether you've previously taken Empirici Academy courses. We welcome new community members.

Where can I learn more about DataCamp Donates?

Visit the official DataCamp Donates page to learn about the program and see success stories from other scholars.
