DataCamp Scholarship

One-year access to DataCamp's complete learning platform for motivated learners.

Empirici Academy has partnered with DataCamp Donates to provide motivated learners with one-year access to DataCamp's complete learning platform. Build job-ready skills in data science, analytics, and AI.

Full Course Access

Complete catalog of courses, career tracks, and skill assessments in Python, R, SQL, Excel, and more.

Hands-On Projects

Interactive coding exercises and real-world projects to build practical, job-ready skills.

Certifications

Industry-recognized certificates for completed tracks to showcase your expertise.

Community Support

Access to Empirici Academy's learning community and expert guidance.

Eligibility

Students

Currently enrolled in university or recent graduates seeking data skills.

Career Changers

Professionals transitioning into data-focused roles.

Self-Learners

Motivated individuals committed to building technical skills independently.

Underrepresented Groups

Individuals from backgrounds underrepresented in tech and data fields.

Requirements: Commitment to actively using the platform, basic English proficiency, and access to a computer with internet connection.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the scholarship last?

The scholarship provides one year of full access to DataCamp from the date of activation.

Is there a cost to apply?

No, the application and scholarship are completely free.

Can I renew after one year?

Renewals are considered on a case-by-case basis depending on engagement and available spots.

What happens if I'm not selected?

You're welcome to reapply in future rounds. We'll provide feedback when possible.

Do I need prior coding experience?

No prior experience is required. DataCamp offers courses for all skill levels from beginner to advanced.

Ready to Start Learning?

Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis. Apply now to be considered for the next cohort.
